Spaghetti Bolognese Recipe

Spaghetti Bolognese is a traditional Italian pasta dish loved all around the world. It features perfectly cooked spaghetti topped with a rich and flavorful meat sauce made from minced beef, tomatoes, onions, garlic, and a blend of herbs and spices. The slow-cooked sauce gives it a deep, comforting taste that makes every bite unforgettable â€” a true classic of Italian cuisine!

Ingredients:

  • 400g (14 oz) spaghetti
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 400g (14 oz) minced beef
  • 1 carrot, finely chopped
  • 1 celery stick, finely chopped
  • 400g (14 oz) canned chopped tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley (for garnish)
  • Grated Parmesan cheese (optional)
